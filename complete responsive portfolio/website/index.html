About Me

Aspiring Web Developer

My love for technology began in 2012, however, I knew I wanted to pursure a career in Information Techology in 2014 after watching the movie Big Hero 6. I was fascinated by the students who applied at the Tech Academy and some the amazing things they could do using technology. However, the one applicant that stood out for me was this young boy who was inspired by his brother to apply at the Academy. I was drawn by how this boy could make use of microbats, swarms of tiny robots that could connect in any configuration using a neurocranial transmitter. I then took it upon myself to apply at WeThinkCode_ and I made it through and I am now a Software Developer.
